प्रमाणिक OTT — मुनि श्री प्रमाणसागर जी महाराज के प्रवचन, भावना योग, शंका समाधान एवं अन्य आध्यात्मिक सामग्री

Pramanik OTT is the official app for spiritual content by Muni Shri 108 Pramansagar Ji Maharaj. Watch daily discourses, Bhawna Yog sessions, Q&A (Shanka Samadhan), Agam Swadhyay, and much more — all in one place.

🕉 Features:
• Daily Pravachan (Discourses) — latest and archive by month
• Bhawna Yog — guided meditation sessions
• Shanka Samadhan — Q&A clips and full episodes
• Agam Swadhyay — scripture study series
• Jain Pathshala — animated stories and live classes for kids
• Poojan & Path — Nitya Poojan, Stotra, Bhajan, Granth Vachan
• Live Events — Vidhaan, Panchkalyanak and more
• Swa Par Kalyan — Support Gaushala, Gunayatan, Gurukula

📱 App Highlights:
• Works on both Mobile and Android TV
• Native video player — no ads, no distractions
• Resume from where you left off
• Auto-play next video in a series
• Continue Watching — pick up where you stopped
• Bilingual — Hindi & English
• Beautiful dark theme with modern UI
• Offline-friendly — cached content loads fast

🎓 Jain Pathshala:
• Animated stories for children
• Weekly live class schedule
• Multi-language support (Hindi, English, Marathi)
• Join live via Zoom → YouTube stream

🔔 Stay Updated:
• Live stream notifications
• Event announcements
• Daily quotes and inspiration
